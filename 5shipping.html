--- Shipping

We typically ship within 24 hours of placing your order; however, some orders may take up to 48 hours to process. Please note that we do not ship on Saturdays or Sundays.All orders will arrive within 3-5 business days.

We offer complimentary shipping worldwide. However, we don’t ship to countries that are not operated by DHL. All orders are shipped and tracked via DHL.

Man Fashion is not responsible for any delays caused by the customs clearance process, or taxation in overseas countries.

Man Fashion is not responsible for lost package due to incorrect address supplied by the sender.

For more information and shipping inquiries contact: connect@manfashion.com

--- Returns process

Change of heart? No problem, we accept exchange or refunds within 30 days after delivery.

You purchase should be returned in saleable condition with all product packaging (box, bag and care card). Insured that the item is safely wrapped in its cloth to avoid scratches. Any damage caused by negligence in the return process will be the customer’s responsibility.

Goods will be refunded upon inspection on arrival (excluding the original delivery charge). Please note that resized items, customized, damaged or otherwise altered after delivery will not be accepted for return. Return shipping charges are not refundable.

To determine the best return process, please contact us at: connect@manfashion.com
